ERNAKULAM :

 Ernakulam is the green commercial capital of Kerala with one of the finest natural harbours in the world.
From here, ships set sail for foreign ports with pepper, sea food, rubber and coir.
From times immemorial, Arabs, Chinese, Dutch, British and Portuguese seafarers followed the sea route to Cochin and left their impression in the town.
The harbour is the nucleus around which Cochin has grown to become the Queen of the Arabian Sea.
